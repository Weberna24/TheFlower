Curious About The Flower? We’ve Got Answers!

We know you might have a few questions before placing your order—and we love that! At The Flower, we believe every bouquet should feel personal and special, so we’re here to make sure you feel confident, excited, and fully informed. Whether you're wondering about custom designs, delivery, pricing, or how our crochet magic works, this is the place to find the answers.

Can I the order?

Definitely, you can customize the bouquet. We offer a variety of flowers and colors for you to choose from, allowing you to create a personalized arrangement just for you. Feel free to DM us with your specific preferences and and any inquries you may have.

Can I request specific colors for my bouquet?

Absolutely! We welcome color preferences to match your unique style. Just let us know when placing the order.

Do I have to pre-order?

Yes! Customers must place a pre-order at least five days in advance. Alternatively, we do have a section of pre-made bouquets available. Please DM us for further details.

Is a deposit required for pre-ordering?

Yes! Deposit is required for the pre-order. It would be 30% of the value of your order.
